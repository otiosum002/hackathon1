Discover Local Cuisine

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a dish of tender lamb cooked in a rich and spicy sauce, characteristic of Kashmiri cuisine. Enjoy it with naan or rice.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a savory crepe made from rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato mixture. It's a favorite breakfast dish in South India.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a tomato-based sauce. It's best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature features spicy chickpeas served with deep-fried bread. Itâ€™s a popular choice for a hearty breakfast or lunch.

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish cooked with spices, meat, or vegetables. Each region has its own variation, including Hyderabadi and Lucknowi.

Pani Puri

Pani Puri is a favorite street food made of crispy puris filled with spicy water, tamarind chutney, potatoes, and chickpeas.

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer features spinach cooked with cottage cheese in a flavorful sauce. It's commonly enjoyed with roti or rice.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a steamed savory cake made from fermented rice and chickpea flour. It's a popular snack from Gujarat.

Ras Malai

Ras Malai is a popular Indian dessert made from soft cheese dumplings soaked in sweet, creamy milk flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a popular street food from Mumbai, featuring a spicy potato fritter served in a bun, often enjoyed with chutneys and fried green chilies.
